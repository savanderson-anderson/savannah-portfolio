About Me

Rooted in story, drawn to people.

Savannah Anderson was born in Lancaster, California and raised in Los Angeles. She is of mixed descent — her father is African American and her mother is Peruvian.

Savannah is an emerging actor with a natural on‑camera presence and a passion for storytelling. With experience in independent projects and self‑driven creative work, she is driven to bring authenticity, curiosity, and emotional honesty to every role.

Her approach to acting, dancing and other art forms is shaped by real‑life experiences, observations, and a genuine love for character‑driven narratives. Throughout her journey, Savannah continues to draw inspiration from God, the people she encounters, the stories she observes, personal passions, and new artistic explorations — all of which fuel her growth as a creative and her pursuit of roles that challenge and excite her.
